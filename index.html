Why this app exists

Instagram's built-in DM search is notoriously bad — it only matches the start of words, ignores context, and often just doesn't return anything. This tool indexes every message in your export so you can find anything anyone ever said to you, in milliseconds.

  • Instant full-text search across every conversation
  • Per-person, per-date-range, and phrase filters
  • Jump-to-context: see the surrounding messages around any hit
  • Never leaves your device. Ever.

How to export your Instagram chats

Instagram calls this "Download your information." You'll request an archive, wait for the email, then extract the ZIP and load it here.

Step 1 — Open the download tool

  1. Open Instagram in a web browser and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Accounts Center (left sidebar, bottom).
  3. Click Your information and permissions → Download your information.
  4. Click Download or transfer information, pick your account, and continue.

Step 2 — Choose what to export

  1. Pick Some of your information.
  2. Scroll down to the Your Instagram activity section and select only Messages. (You can include more if you want — the app ignores anything that isn't a message.)
  3. Click Next.

Step 3 — Set the format

  • Download to device.
  • Date range: All time.
  • Format: JSON. This is critical — HTML exports will not work.
  • Media quality: Low (faster download; the app is text-focused).
  • Click Create files.

Step 4 — Wait for Instagram's email

Instagram will email you a download link when the archive is ready. This usually takes minutes but can take up to 48 hours for large accounts. Download the ZIP from that email link.

Step 5 — Extract the ZIP

  • Windows: right-click the ZIP → Extract All…
  • macOS: double-click the ZIP.
  • Linux: unzip instagram-*.zip

Step 6 — Load it here

Go back to the Search tab and drop the extracted folder onto the upload box. The app will find the inbox automatically. For best performance, you can also drag just the your_instagram_activity/messages/inbox folder.

Reminder: everything parses on your device. You could physically disconnect from the internet at this point — the app would still work.

Privacy & security

This app is engineered so your Instagram data cannot leave your device, even if it wanted to.

Hard guarantees

  • No servers. The app is static files. There is no backend to send data to.
  • No network requests. A Content Security Policy (connect-src 'none') blocks the browser from making any outbound fetch, XHR, or WebSocket connection from this page.
  • No third-party scripts. No analytics, no CDNs, no trackers. Everything is local.
  • No persistence by default. Messages live in RAM only. Close the tab and they're gone.
  • Works fully offline. Load the page once, then switch to airplane mode. Everything still works.

How to verify

  1. Open your browser's DevTools → Network tab.
  2. Load this page and use the app normally.
  3. You'll see zero requests after the initial asset load.
  4. Or just turn off Wi-Fi entirely — the app still works.

Clear your data

Everything you loaded is cleared when you close or refresh the tab. You can also click the button below to wipe the in-memory index immediately.
